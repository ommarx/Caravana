Talk to the team →
Is Caravana genuinely free to start?
+
Yes — the Essential plan is free forever. Full access to all comparison tools, five repair quotes a month, and community support. No credit card required, ever.
How is Caravana unbiased if providers pay you?
+
Providers pay performance-based referral fees only after you choose them — never before. No provider can pay to rank higher or influence your results in any way.
Are the mechanic quotes legally binding?
+
Every quote from our verified network is locked for 48 hours. If your final bill exceeds the quoted amount without prior written approval, we cover the difference up to ₹2,000.
Which cities is Caravana currently live in?
+
Currently live in Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, Pune, Hyderabad, and Chennai. Expanding to 12 more cities by Q3 2025 — enter your city in the form and we'll notify you.
Can I switch or cancel my plan at any time?
+
Absolutely. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel directly from your dashboard at any point. No lock-in periods, no cancellation penalties — ever.
How accurate are the insurance comparisons?
+
Quotes are pulled live via direct insurer APIs — not cached data. What you see is exactly what you'll pay, down to the rupee, at that moment.
